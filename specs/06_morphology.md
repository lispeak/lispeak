# Morphology

> When everyone knows beauty as beautiful, there is already ugliness; When everyone knows good as goodness, there is already evil. To be and not to be arise mutually
> **Lao Tzu**

Most of the words have symmetric oppositions:

- beauty/ugliness
- left/right
- warm/cold
- high/low
- object/subject
- smart/stupid

So in Lispeak all this oppositions are made out of the same root but use different letters.

"Left" letters: `u`, `e`, `yu`, `ye`, `p`, `t`, `k`, `c`, `f`, `s`, `x`, `n`, `h`.
"Right" letters: `a`, `o`, `ya`, `yo`, `b`, `d`, `g`, `j`, `v`, `z`, `l`, `m`, `r`.
"Neutral" letters: `i`.

It makes "right" words sound good and "left" words sound bad. Just check the examples:

```
Left:  puhenuc
Right: baromaj
```
